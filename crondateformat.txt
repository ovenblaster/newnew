The CRON job date format

1	minute			0-59 or *
2	hour			0-23 or *
3	day of the month	1-31 or *
4	month			1-12 or *
5	day of the week		0-7 or *

example cron time string 0 10 15 * * . This command would execute on the 15th day of each month at 10:00 am UTC.

0 0 * * * run a job every day at midnight.

5 2 2 1 5  at 2:05 on 2nd day of the month and on friday in january
